
# Login

When you access the portal, you’ll be prompted with the **Log In** screen.  
Here, you can enter your **username** and **password**, then tap **Log In** to access your account.  
This is the same login you use for the mobile app, after onboarding has been complete and Jake has set up your admin account.<br>

# Refund Dashboard
Clicking the refunds tab on the side bar brings you here. Here you can  
approve or deny refunds. <br>
