# Log In

When you open the app, you’ll be prompted with the **Log In** screen.  
Here, you can enter your **username** and **password**, then tap **Log In** to access your account.  
If you don’t have an account yet, tap **Create an Account** to register.

# Sign Up

If you don’t have an account, click the **Create Account** button on the Log In screen.  
Enter the required information, then tap **Sign Up** to create your account and access the application.

# QR Scanner

After logging in, you’ll be directed to the main screen.  
Tap **Start Scanning** to activate your camera and scan a QR code.

  <img width="489" height="838" alt="QR Scanner Screen" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5bfd7cff-2351-4b3e-b7d5-446073dca9bc" />
